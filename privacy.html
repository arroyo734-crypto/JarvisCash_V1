Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 11, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Jarvis Cash 2.0 collects the following information:

  • Wallet Address: When you connect your crypto wallet via MetaMask, we store your wallet address locally in your browser's localStorage.
  • Usage Data: We track button clicks and revenue increments for your personal dashboard.
  • Browser Information: Standard web logs including IP address, browser type, and access times.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our cryptocurrency wallet connection feature
  • Track and display your personal revenue counter
  • Improve our services based on usage patterns
  • Ensure the security and integrity of our platform

3. Data Storage and Security

All sensitive data is stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage. We do not transmit this data to our servers.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your data stored in localStorage.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at our website.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@jarviscash.example
