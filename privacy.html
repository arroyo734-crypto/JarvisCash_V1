Privacy Policy

1. Overview

Our service (the “YouTube Content Generation Machine”) helps you create and manage video content. This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account data: email address, name, and any other data you provide when you sign up.
  • Usage data: IP address, device information, and interaction logs (e.g., pages visited, features used).
  • Content metadata: titles, descriptions, and thumbnails you submit to generate videos.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the service.
  • Process video‑generation requests and personalize content recommendations.
  • Communicate about updates, security incidents, or new features.
  • Analyze usage trends to detect bugs and improve performance.

4. Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share it with:

  • Service providers that help us host, run analytics, or process payments (they are bound by confidentiality).
  • Law‑enforcement or regulatory authorities when required by law.
  • Business partners for joint marketing purposes only if you explicitly opt‑in.

5. Data Retention

Account data is retained until you delete your account. Usage logs are kept for a maximum of 12 months before being anonymized or deleted.

6. Security

We employ industry‑standard TLS encryption, secure storage, and regular vulnerability assessments to protect your information.

7. Your Rights

  • Access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data.
  • Export a copy of your data in a portable format.
  • Withdraw consent for any non‑essential processing at any time.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our service is not directed at persons under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be posted at your-domain.com/privacy.html.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns, please contact us at privacy@your-domain.com.
